Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy
At AxelLab, we believe privacy is not a feature—it's a principle. The Hemingway-Style Readability Checker is built on a stateless, client-side architecture that ensures your data remains entirely yours. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your content never leaves your browser.


Stateless by Design
This utility operates without accounts, cookies, or server-side processing. Every analysis is performed locally, and every result is generated in real time within your device. No logs, no tracking, no external dependencies. This stateless model eliminates risk and reinforces user autonomy.


Zero Data Collection
We do not collect or retain any personally identifiable information (PII). There are no analytics scripts, no behavioral profiling, and no third-party integrations. Your writing, usage patterns, and device data are never accessed or stored by us.


Transparency and Reproducibility
All readability scoring is based on deterministic, rule-based formulas such as Flesch-Kincaid, Gunning Fog, and ARI. These methods are fully documented and reproducible, ensuring that users understand exactly how their content is evaluated.


Accessibility and Compliance
We prioritize inclusive design. The interface supports keyboard navigation, screen readers, and high-contrast themes. Our stateless architecture aligns with global privacy standards including GDPR, CCPA, and POPIA, without requiring formal consent mechanisms—because we never process personal data.


Why This Matters
In an era of opaque AI models and invasive cloud services, our tool offers a principled alternative. It’s built for writers, educators, editors, and compliance professionals who value clarity, control, and privacy. No surveillance. No compromise.
